What is Korean Cuisine?

1

History

Korean cuisine, with its centuries-old history, emphasizes balance and seasonal variety. Key elements include rice, vegetables, meats, and iconic side dishes like kimchi. The use of unique seasonings contributes to the distinct flavors of Korean dishes

2

How to enjoy Korean Cuisine?


Enjoying Korean cuisine involves using chopsticks or spoons to savor a variety of dishes. Try mixing rice and various side dishes like kimchi on your plate for a harmonious blend of flavors. Use chopsticks to pick up meat or veggies, and don't forget to pair your meal with traditional Korean sauces for an authentic experience

SIDE DISH


Enjoy Korean cuisine with our traditional side dishes called Banchan!

김치(Kimchi)


Kimchi is a traditional Korean side dish made of fermented vegetables, usually cabbage or radishes, with chili peppers and other seasonings.

콩자반(Kongjaban)


Kongjaban is a Korean side dish made with seasoned soybeans, providing a sweet and savory flavor.

숙주나물(Sukju namul)


Sukju namul is a Korean side dish made from mung bean sprouts, blanched and seasoned for a refreshing and crunchy taste.

Opening Our Fifth Korean
Restaurant
in Cluj-Napoca, Romania!

I am thrilled to announce the opening of our fifth restaurant in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. It brings me great joy to see more people in Romania experiencing Korean cuisine and learning about Korean culture. Our restaurant is designed with the motif of a traditional Korean house, known as "hanok." Here, you can not only savor delicious Korean dishes but also immerse yourself in the charm of Korean traditional architecture.

We are dedicated to providing a space where you can enjoy not only tasty food but also the beauty of Korea's rich heritage. Beyond the culinary experience, we invite you to explore and appreciate the traditional aspects of Korean culture. Thank you for your continued support, and we look forward to serving you at our new location!
